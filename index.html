How Important is Mental Health?

Mental health is crucial for overall well-being, impacting how we think, feel, and live. Prioritizing it promotes resilience, balance, and healthier lives.
Signs That You need help

Signs that someone may need help include feeling overwhelmed by sadness or worry, withdrawing from loved ones, losing interest in things they once enjoyed, or struggling to find the energy to face each day. Changes in sleep, appetite, or mood, along with feelings of hopelessness or being unable to cope, are gentle reminders that it’s okay to seek support—you don’t have to face it alone.
