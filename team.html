Join our Team

Are you a passionate and qualified counsellor looking to expand your practice and connect with more clients? Join our growing network of counsellors and be part of a team dedicated to making affordable mental health support accessible to everyone.

Why Join Our Network?

  • Client Referrals: We handle client referrals, ensuring a steady stream of potential clients. Each session costs R480, of which you will receive R320.
  • Mentorship Support: Access experienced mentors who can guide you with challenging cases or provide professional advice when needed.
  • Administrative Relief: Focus on what you do best—counselling. We handle invoicing and payment collection on your behalf.
  • Seamless Payments: Rest assured, you will be paid for every session with minimal effort on your part.
  • Flexibility: Work from your chosen online platform. While we recommend Zoom, which you will need to have a paid account for (sessions are 50 minutes long), we are open to discussing alternatives.
  • Build Your Practice: Grow your client base and professional reputation while working in a supportive, collaborative environment.
  • Professional Networking: Be part of a community of like-minded professionals, creating opportunities for collaboration, learning, and growth.
  • Minimal Marketing Hassles: We promote the service and attract clients for you, so you can focus entirely on providing exceptional counselling.
  • Convenience: Manage your sessions remotely from the comfort of your home or office.
  • Commitment to Excellence: Join a team that prioritizes high-quality service, ensuring every client receives professional and compassionate support.

Requirements to Join:

  • Hold qualifications that are in alignment with counselling or psychotherapy.
  • Be a registered member of a recognized professional board (e.g., HPCSA, SACSSP, or an equivalent organization).
  • Possess a paid Zoom account (or discuss other online platforms with us) to facilitate 50-minute sessions.

How to Apply:

  1. Send an email to affordablecounselling2@gmail.com expressing your interest.
  2. We will send you a form to complete with your details and qualifications.
  3. Once we review your application, you will be contacted by either Quinton Mundell or Jean-Marie Olwage for a discussion.
  4. Following the evaluation, we will inform you of our decision regarding your inclusion in the network.

Take the next step in your counselling career. Be part of a team that values your skills and helps you make a meaningful difference in people’s lives while growing your professional practice.
